Instructions

  • 1. Start the game and control the snake using the arrow keys on your keyboard.
  • 2. Eat as much food as possible without hitting the walls or the snake's own body.
  • 3. Each time you eat a piece of food, the snake grows longer and your score increases.
  • 4. The game is over if the snake runs into a wall or its own body.
  • 5. Try to get the highest score possible!

That's it! Good luck and have fun playing the classic game of Snake!
